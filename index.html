Work process

Planning and Preparation

1

I begin a QA task by thoroughly reviewing the requirements, including user stories and specifications, to develop a comprehensive test strategy that identifies necessary testing types. Following this, I design detailed test cases and establish the test environment, ensuring all tools and data are prepared for efficient execution.

Execution and Documentation

2

I meticulously execute each test case, documenting the results and logging any issues encountered with comprehensive bug reports that include steps to reproduce. Upon receiving fixes from the development team, I diligently verify their effectiveness and ensure they don't introduce new issues, maintaining the integrity of the software.

Analysis and Reporting

3

I thoroughly analyze the test results to gain a comprehensive understanding of the software's quality, generating detailed reports that summarize test outcomes, bug statistics, and overall quality assessment. I then effectively communicate these findings to the team, highlighting critical issues and risks, while providing actionable recommendations for further testing and process improvements.
